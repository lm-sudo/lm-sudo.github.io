    About Me

    Hello! My name is Louis Medina, I am a graduate of the University of California - Irvine class of 2019. I am currently a software developer where I provide support in database management, create and edit a variety of web pages on both the front-end and back-end, and regularly meet with C-level officers to discuss both ongoing and future programming projects.

    As an aspiring full-stack developer I primarily write PHP, SQL(postgres) and JavaScript code. In addition to attaining mastery in what I already practice, I would also like to spend time to develop my skills in Python and perhaps take up new languages like TypeScript, Java, and R. While my focus is on programming, a wide range of tech-related fields interest me, including: cyber security, server management, cryptography, machine learning, and highly technical endeavors like kernel programming.

    My current goals are to further develop my skill set and cultivate a deeper understanding of software architecture with regards to planning, development, and maintenance of programs. Beyond my current goals, I aspire to become a competent full-stack developer and then later, a senior software architect.
